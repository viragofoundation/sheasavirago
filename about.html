Welcome to Virago Foundation, a dedicated NGO focused on empowering and uplifting amateur women entrepreneurs in India. We believe that every woman possesses immense potential and the ability to create a positive impact on society. Our mission is to provide comprehensive support and resources to help these aspiring entrepreneurs turn their dreams into reality.
Through mentorship programs, skill-building workshops, access to funding, and a strong network of like-minded individuals, we aim to nurture and develop their entrepreneurial talents.Join us in this transformative journey as we inspire, equip, and empower the next generation of women entrepreneurs in India.
At Virago Foundation, we envision a future where women entrepreneurs play a vital role in shaping India's economic landscape, driving innovation, and fostering social change.
