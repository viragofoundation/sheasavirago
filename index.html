Welcome to Virago Foundation, a dedicated NGO focused on empowering and uplifting amateur women entrepreneurs in India. We believe that every woman possesses immense potential and the ability to create a positive impact on society. Our mission is to provide comprehensive support and resources to help these aspiring entrepreneurs turn their dreams into reality.
Through mentorship programs, skill-building workshops, access to funding, and a strong network of like-minded individuals, we aim to nurture and develop their entrepreneurial talents. Join us in this transformative journey as we inspire, equip, and empower the next generation of women entrepreneurs in India.
At Virago Foundation, we envision a future where women entrepreneurs play a vital role in shaping India's economic landscape, driving innovation, and fostering social change.

Become A Volunteer

Let’s make a difference in the lives of others

Calling all passionate individuals who are eager to make a difference! Virago Foundation is seeking volunteers to join our team and help us empower amateur women entrepreneurs in India.If you have a deep commitment to women's empowerment and want to contribute your skills and time towards a meaningful cause, we invite you to become a volunteer with us. As a volunteer, you will have the opportunity to directly impact the lives of aspiring women entrepreneurs, providing them with the support and guidance they need to succeed. Whether you have expertise in business, marketing, finance, or a strong desire to help others, we welcome individuals from all backgrounds. Your unique skills and experiences will play a vital role in creating a nurturing environment where these talented women can grow and thrive.
